Welcome

I am a Senior Lecturer in the School of Accounting, Auditing & Taxation at the UNSW Business School, UNSW Sydney. My research focuses on financial accounting, shareholder litigation, stock price crash risk, and voluntary disclosure.

I completed my Ph.D. in Accounting at UNSW Sydney and hold prior degrees from the Australian National University and Shanghai University of Finance and Economics.
